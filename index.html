Welcome

Discover Products

Prabha Automotive Engineers is the flagship company of the Prabha Group. The Prabha Group has over five decades of presence in the Indian auto industry, catering to sheet metal requirements in the truck, two wheeler & passenger car segments.

With its committed engineering team & highly skilled workforce, Prabha Automotive Engineers is a tier 1 ancillary to Ashok Leyland, with proven leadership in supply of fully trimmed modules like truck cabins, Front End Structures (FES), tipper bodies, and stamping weldments.

Prabha's journey began from a modest shed in 1970. Over the years, it has become a leading ancillary in the Indian commercial vehicle industry with thirteen manufacturing locations spread over India.
